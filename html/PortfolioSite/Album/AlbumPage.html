Meteora

Linkin Park

Release Date: March 25, 2003

Meteora is the second studio album by American rock band Linkin Park produced by the band alongside Don Gilmore.
The title Meteora is taken from the Greek Orthodox monasteries sharing the same name.

Band Members

  • Chester Bennington – Vocals
  • Mike Shinoda – Vocals, rap vocals, rhythm guitar, keyboard, piano, samples, strings arrangement on "Faint" and "Breaking the Habit"
  • Brad Delson – Lead guitar
  • Dave "Phoenix" Farrell – Bass guitar
  • Mr. Hahn – Turntables, samples, programming
  • Rob Bourdon – Drums, percussion
